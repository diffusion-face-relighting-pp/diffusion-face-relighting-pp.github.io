Additional Relighting Results

We present additional relighting results featuring diverse lighting paths. These input images contain existing strong highlights and cast shadows, a wide range of head poses, or facial makeup and accessories. On the left, we visualize the approximate location of the dominant light source, where the size of the red circle represents the area of the light source. Smaller areas produce sharper cast shadows, while larger areas create more diffuse appearances.
